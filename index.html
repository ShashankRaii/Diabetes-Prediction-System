Measures for
Managing Diabetes

  1. 01

    Follow a healthy diet: Eating a well-balanced diet that is low in sugar and refined carbohydrates can help manage blood glucose levels. Focus on whole foods like fruits, vegetables, whole grains, lean proteins, and healthy fats.

  2. 02

    Exercise regularly: Regular exercise can help improve insulin sensitivity and help manage blood glucose levels. Aim for at least 30 minutes of moderate-intensity exercise most days of the week.

  3. 03

    Monitor blood glucose levels: Regularly checking blood glucose levels can help you track how well your diabetes management plan is working and make necessary adjustments.

  4. 04

    Take medication as prescribed: If prescribed medication to manage diabetes, it's important to take it as directed by your healthcare provider.
